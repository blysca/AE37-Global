Managing Partner: Olexandr Chernyak

Bio: AE37 Global is the company, founded and managed by Olexandr Chernyak, who is a well-known entrepreneur and investor in the industry. Olexandr’s past experience includes founding and managing Carprice and Smartprice. These two companies became the leaders of its industries in Russia and CIS countries. Carprice employs more than 700 people and has an annual revenue of $250m, its Japanese subsidiary was successfully sold to Japanese company Rakuten. Smartprice is the leader in ever-green smartphone reselling market of Russia. The company sells almost 500,000 smartphones per year and employs more than 130 people.

Besides the entrepreneurial success, Olexandr is also an investor and advisor. His portfolio companies represent key players of the startup industry, top incubator graduates and promising teams, such as Allset (United States foodtech company, backed by Andreessen Horowitz), Foodfox (Russian foodtech company sold to Yandex), Mobalytics (analytical e-gaming platform backed by GGV and Founders Fund), Rapyd (global fintech solution valued at almost $800m), Reef Technologies (largest operator and owner of parking lots in the Unites States). In these companies Olexandr is helping founders to manage the business, grow the sales, update the processes, and do the fundraising. Besides these companies, Olexandr is also an investor in Zinvo (US watches company), Instacarro (Brazilian car auction), Shedul (European booking platform).
